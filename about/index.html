Background

I grew up in rural New Zealand, with two lower-middle-class, hard-working parents and I was one of six children. I was raised with a great work ethic, good values, and the understanding that to get anywhere in life - you had to work hard.

I started working in recruitment straight out of University while living in New Zealand. Once I finished my degree I moved to London, England. I then went on to work in Talent Acquisition (aka recruitment) for over 20+ years across 40+ industries and 40+ countries. I have been in Switzerland since 2016 and prior to that I lived in England and New Zealand.
