The Early Years

I grew up in rural New Zealand, with two lower-middle-class, hard-working parents and I was one of seven children.

As a child I grew up in large loving family living in number of different small towns across New Zealand.  My parents often worked two jobs to support all 7 children and a lot of my life lessons were taught to me by my siblings.

My Career

I started working in recruitment straight out of University while living in New Zealand.

Once I finished my degree I asked my professor how can I apply the skills I have learnt at University.  He said the best way is in a company’s Human Resources department and the best way to get there is via Recruitment.  

The rest is history.

Talent Acquisition

I have since gone on to work in Talent Acquisition (aka recruitment) for over 20+ years.

I have been fortunate enough to travel the world and be exposed to various cultures across the globe with my work.
